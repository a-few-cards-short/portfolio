Welcome to my portfolio. My name is Cody and I'm seeking work in post-secondary instruction for digital media and software development. I'm a jack-of-all-trades, master of at least a couple of things ... probably. ;-)

One of my favourite thinkers is Dieter Rams; he truly understands the "science" of design. His 10 principles of design is a must read for anyone creating products (even digital ones).

My modus operandi approach to development is to keep things simple and apply complexity only through necessity. It's very easy to overthink things (guilty as charged) or believe that things are more complex than they really are, but recognizing this is an important skill to develop for any endeavour. Providing clarity is where I put most of my effort into and I believe that translates well to instruction.

I hope you enjoy my thoughts and insights.

Also, I hand-coded this website so ... yay me!  :-)
