logo design

Logo design is about being distinct and clear. Complexity distracts and becomes noise to our minds. Simplicity is not about taking shortcuts and being less effective; it's about editing an idea down to present the clearest message possible.

Note: "IF" stands for interactive fiction (a videogame genre that embraces text as the medium). I didn't mispell infinity.  ;-)

All logos, except one, were done at no charge simply because I hate seeing great ideas exist solely in 12pt Arial.

technical drawing

Illustrating with accuracy and simplicity is necessary for technical drawing. I was tasked with providing lasering artwork to wrap around a large Yeti mug. It was important that the massive 3000Hp hydraulic shovel be that exact 6060 model. All I could find online was a JPG schematic, but the line art was too busy and too thin to show up well as a finished product. This kind of technical art lends well to design documentation and especially UI wireframing.

Wireframing is the foundation of all UI design and super simple to produce. All programmers should be familiar and capable of UI box-wireframing and flowcharting.
